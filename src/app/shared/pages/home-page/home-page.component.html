Country App

An application to find information from any country in the world!

Discover the world in the palm of your hand with our app, the global compass for the curious. Browse through three sections: search by country, capital or region and discover fascinating facts. See the flags, learn about the population and dive into unique details, such as the translation of the country's name in several languages. A must-have tool for knowledge explorers, students or travelers. Explore, learn and be amazed by our galaxy of geographic data!
